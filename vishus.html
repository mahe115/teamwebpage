Vishnu's Role - Technical Test Resources

Role Details

Vishnu focuses on technical test resources. He curates a collection of data structure and algorithm (DSA) problems that are often presented in technical interview rounds. His efforts equip the team with the skills and confidence to excel in the technical aspects of interviews.

Process of the Role

Vishnu's process involves:

  • Researching common technical interview topics
  • Collecting a variety of DSA problems
  • Organizing problems by difficulty and category
  • Updating the problem bank regularly

Outcomes of the Role

The outcomes of Vishnu's role include:

  • Providing team members with a strong technical foundation
  • Boosting problem-solving skills and algorithmic thinking
  • Enhancing confidence in technical interview scenarios
